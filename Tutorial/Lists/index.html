Dragons A dragon is a mythical creature found in the folklore of cultures throughout the world. It is roughly serpentine in form, and often possesses features drawn from different animals, such as wings and claws. In nearly all cultures, it is conceived of as a powerful and awe-inspiring creature.

vampire
A mythical, undead creature that survives by feeding on the vital essence (usually blood) of the living. Stemming from Eastern European folklore, these figures are traditionally depicted as reanimated corpses that sleep in coffins, possess fangs, and transform into bats.
werewolf
A mythical creature in European folklore capable of shapeshifting into a wolf or a hybrid wolf-human.
